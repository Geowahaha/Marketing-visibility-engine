Frequently Asked Questions

What does the AI Mark proof demo verify?

It verifies that AI Mark can save a weak baseline, publish an improved page, rescan the same public URL, and report before-after score movement with an honest proof link.

Why is before-after proof important for SMEs?

SMEs often spend scarce budget on ads before the website is clear enough for customers, Google, and AI answer engines. Before-after proof helps show whether fixes changed public visibility signals before the business spends more on promotion.

Does this demo promise rankings or revenue?

No. The demo proves observed public visibility signals only. It does not guarantee Google ranking, AI citation, traffic, leads, or revenue.

What should be checked after applying fixes?

Check that the live HTML contains the new metadata, schema, FAQ content, robots.txt, llms.txt, and social previews. Then rerun AI Mark Proof and compare the result with the saved baseline.
