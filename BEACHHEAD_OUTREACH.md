  meruaccounting) for *"bookkeeping Thailand small business."* It points to **Reddit,
  Facebook, Stripe** before any Thai accountant. **Acclime wins ~5 of 8 queries.**
- Pinpoint is **invisible** to the buyers who are *actively asking AI to recommend an
  accountant right now.* Every one of those is a lost ฿15k–50k engagement.


## The 4-step first-call flow (same for any prospect)

1. **Probe live** — run AIMark on their site; show who AI recommends *instead* of them.
2. **Why they lose** — "Why competitors win" → the specific gaps + the fixes.
3. **The offer** — Growth Monitor **฿490/mo**: monitoring + alerts + the fix roadmap;
   *"we log your real leads/sales so you see exactly which fix made you money."*
4. **Close now** — open `aimark.pages.dev/dashboard` → Connect site → **Subscribe**
   (card recurring, or PromptPay 30-day to start today). The checkout is live and works.

## After they pay (so retention + P5 data become real)
- Log their first leads/sales in the **outcome panel** → AIMark shows *"the fix you did →
  ฿X revenue"* → that single screen is what makes month 2 a no-brainer.
- That same data is the first row of the Revenue Intelligence dataset (P5) — which only
  becomes real once a paying customer's outcomes flow in.
